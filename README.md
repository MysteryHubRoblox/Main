<h2>📜 Our Pledge</h2>

<p>
As members, contributors, and maintainers of <strong>Mystery Hub</strong>, we pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free, respectful, and welcoming experience for everyone — regardless of age, skill level, background, identity, or experience.
</p>

<p>
<strong>Mystery Hub is a scripting project built for the Roblox platform.</strong> Our community exists to support the development, improvement, and responsible use of our scripts and tools across supported Roblox experiences.
</p>

<p>
We are committed to fostering an open, inclusive, and healthy environment where users, contributors, and developers can collaborate productively and grow together.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>✅ Our Standards</h2>

<h3>Behavior that strengthens our community</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Treating other users, contributors, and developers with respect and patience</li>
  <li>Sharing ideas, feedback, and bug reports in a clear and constructive way</li>
  <li>Helping others learn how to use, configure, and troubleshoot the project</li>
  <li>Keeping discussions focused on code, features, performance, and user experience</li>
  <li>Accepting constructive criticism gracefully and using it to improve</li>
  <li>Giving credit where it's due and respecting the work of other contributors</li>
  <li>Acting in the best interest of the community, the project, and its users</li>
</ul>

<h3>Behavior we do not tolerate</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Insults, harassment, mockery, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind</li>
  <li>Trolling, flame-baiting, or deliberately creating conflict</li>
  <li>Sexual, offensive, violent, or otherwise inappropriate language or imagery</li>
  <li>Sharing someone's private information (doxxing) without explicit permission</li>
  <li>Impersonating staff, contributors, or other community members</li>
  <li>Sabotaging the project, submitting malicious code, or intentionally spreading broken builds</li>
  <li>Redistributing the project under a different name or claiming authorship of it</li>
  <li>Any conduct that would be considered unprofessional in a collaborative development setting</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>🛡️ Enforcement Responsibilities</h2>

<p>
The maintainers of <strong>Mystery Hub</strong> are responsible for clarifying and enforcing the standards outlined in this Code of Conduct. They may take any action they deem appropriate and fair in response to behavior they consider inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.
</p>

<p>
Maintainers have the right — and responsibility — to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct. When needed, they will communicate reasoning behind moderation decisions.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>🌐 Scope</h2>

<p>
This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces officially associated with <strong>Mystery Hub</strong>, and also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.
</p>

<p>This includes, but is not limited to:</p>

<ul>
  <li>All GitHub repositories under the Mystery Hub project</li>
  <li>Issues, Pull Requests, Discussions, and code reviews</li>
  <li>The official Discord server and any other affiliated community platforms</li>
  <li>Social media accounts, posts, or communications made on behalf of Mystery Hub</li>
  <li>Any public or private space where Mystery Hub is discussed or represented</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>📢 Reporting</h2>

<p>
If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the maintainers as soon as possible. Reports can be sent privately through the project's official Discord server or via direct message to a staff member.
</p>

<p>All reports will be:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly</li>
  <li>Handled with discretion and respect for the privacy of the reporter</li>
  <li>Acted upon based on the Enforcement Guidelines below</li>
</ul>

<p>
Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and safety of anyone reporting an incident in good faith.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>⚖️ Enforcement Guidelines</h2>

<p>Maintainers will follow these guidelines when determining the consequences for any action they consider a violation of this Code of Conduct.</p>

<h3>1. Correction</h3>
<p>
<strong>Impact:</strong> Use of inappropriate language, unprofessional behavior, or a minor disruption to the community.<br>
<strong>Consequence:</strong> A private written warning from maintainers, clarifying the nature of the violation and explaining why the behavior was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.
</p>

<h3>2. Warning</h3>
<p>
<strong>Impact:</strong> A violation through a single incident or a pattern of repeated minor behavior.<br>
<strong>Consequence:</strong> A formal warning and temporary restriction from interacting with the project or its communities. Continued violations may result in escalated action.
</p>

<h3>3. Temporary Suspension</h3>
<p>
<strong>Impact:</strong> A serious violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior or harassment.<br>
<strong>Consequence:</strong> A temporary ban from all community interaction, contribution, and communication for a specified period. Violating the terms of the ban may lead to permanent removal.
</p>

<h3>4. Permanent Ban</h3>
<p>
<strong>Impact:</strong> Demonstrated pattern of violations, sustained harassment, abuse, or deliberate harm toward the project, its users, or its contributors.<br>
<strong>Consequence:</strong> A permanent removal from all official Mystery Hub spaces, with no path for appeal.
</p>
